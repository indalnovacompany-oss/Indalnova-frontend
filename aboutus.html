About Indalnova

Our Journey: Indalnova was founded at just 16 by Aniket Singh Thakur, driven by a passion to create skincare and luxury perfumes that are authentic, effective, and sophisticated. Tired of shortcuts, false promises, and substandard products in the beauty industry, Aniket set out to craft items that truly deliver results—honest, high-quality, and designed to elevate daily routines into moments of indulgence.

Our Mission: At Indalnova, our mission is simple yet profound: to redefine luxury self-care in India. We combine nature’s purity with scientific innovation to create bathwash, skincare, and perfumes that nurture, refresh, and inspire confidence. Every product we make encourages a lifestyle of self-love, wellness, and elegance.

Our Vision: Our vision is to offer discerning customers premium quality, exceptional fragrances, and skincare that truly cares. Indalnova aims to make indulgence accessible while upholding integrity, sustainability, and safety. We believe that luxury should never compromise authenticity.

Our Values:

  • Exquisite Quality: Every bathwash, perfume, and skincare product is meticulously crafted using premium ingredients.
  • Luxury Experience: Our products transform your daily routine into a sensory journey of elegance and sophistication.
  • Sustainability & Responsibility: From sourcing to packaging, we are eco-conscious and minimize environmental impact.
  • Transparency & Trust: Every formula is clean, cruelty-free, and honest—no shortcuts, no compromises.
  • Customer Delight: We strive to exceed expectations and continuously innovate to ensure satisfaction.

Crafted with Care: Every Indalnova product is plant-based, lightweight, and suitable for all skin types, especially sensitive skin. Our bathwashes envelop you in luxurious textures and captivating fragrances, while our perfumes are curated to leave a lasting impression. Created in small, carefully controlled batches, each product embodies attention to detail, quality, and elegance.

The Indalnova Lifestyle: Indalnova is more than products—it’s a promise of indulgence, confidence, and sophistication. We encourage customers to embrace self-care as a ritual, savoring each moment of fragrance, touch, and glow. Whether it’s the gentle caress of our bathwash or the lingering scent of our perfumes, Indalnova transforms everyday routines into premium experiences.

Founder’s Note: “Indalnova is my vision brought to life: a brand where authenticity meets elegance, and every product tells a story of care, luxury, and refinement. We don’t just create bathwash or perfumes—we craft experiences that inspire confidence and sophistication.”

— Aniket Singh Thakur, Founder of Indalnova
