Track your Order

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an email and/or SMS containing your Tracking ID and shipping details. Please keep this ID safe, as it is required to track your shipment.

How to Track Your Order

1. Enter your Tracking ID in the box provided on this page.

2. Click on the Track Order button.

3. You will be redirected to Shiprocket’s tracking page where you can view the live status of your shipment.

Important Notes

Tracking IDs are generated only after your order is processed and shipped.

You will receive the Tracking ID on your registered Email.

Please allow up to 24–48 hours for the tracking details to reflect after receiving your ID.

Sometimes, tracking updates may take longer depending on the courier partner.

Contact Us

If you face any issues with tracking or have not received your Tracking ID, please contact our support team at: supportindalnova@gmail.com
